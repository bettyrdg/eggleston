Eggleston was founded in 2017 by two friends in Chicago, IL that share a love for breakfast and an appreciate for famous photographer, William Eggleston.

Eggleston is an all vegetarian breakfast restaurant and coffee shop located in northwest Chicago. We specialize in egg dishes and hand-crafted espresso beverages. We also bake fresh pastries every morning, rotating our menu every season with speciality items.

We take much pride in offering a menu of organic, locally sourced dishes. Our eggs come from a free-range chicken farm located an hour from the city, and everything else from our produce, fruit, and espresso beans come from an organic farm that we have built a strong relationship with. Here at Eggleston we are passionate about creating a safe space and welcoming everyone to enjoy our signature Egg dishes and hand-crafted beverages.
